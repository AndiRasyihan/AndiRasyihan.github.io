About Tupperware

Tupperware is a well-known brand name for plastic household appliances, including storage containers, serving containers and some kitchen utensils which were introduced to the general public in 1946.
Tupperware products are made of LDPE and PP (plastic types 4 and 5). These materials are considered safe for use as food containers and can be used repeatedly
